Top three activities to do in Bucharest

Visit the Old Town

You can visit the Old Town and see historical places and as well enjoy the romanian atmosphere while eating delicious foods

Go on a walk on Calea Victoriei

It is known as the prettiest and most loved boulevard in the city, where you can either pass by car, walk or bike, and if you want you can stop for a coffee or gelato

Enjoy Therme

No matter the season, you can go and enjoy Therme since it is an indoor pool where you can choose to have fun in the aqua park zone or the relaxation zone
